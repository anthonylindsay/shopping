Welcome to Shopping!

Thanks for giving Shopping a go - we think you'll like it.

How it works

  1. Use the PLUS button to add an item to your shopping list.
  2. Check the checkbox next to it to cross it off.
  3. Use the CROSS to clear off checked items.
  4. Use the DELETE key to clear the entire list.
  5. Use the COG WHEEL to toggle the menus.

Nifty Features

Shopping remembers what you type, and makes suggestions based upon past items in your list. This autocomplete feature works on categories and items.

We've made no assumptions about how you want to categorise your shopping - you can type in anything. We've included a few suggestions for the autocomplete just to get you going.

Shopping remembers the last category you used for an item. However, if you wish to change an item's category, just tap on it edit away.

Clearing the Cache

Because Shopping remembers everything you type, in theory, if you use it long enough, the app could slow down because of all the stored data. Clearing your cache empties the part of Shopping's memory that is used for the autocomplete and category suggestions. It does not affect your shopping list!
